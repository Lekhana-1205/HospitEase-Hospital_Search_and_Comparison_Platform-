    About HospitEase

    HospitEase is a web application designed to help people quickly find hospitals in their vicinity, especially during emergencies. Our mission is to reduce the time it takes to locate appropriate medical facilities when every second counts.

    Using real-time location data and OpenStreetMap's extensive database, HospitEase provides accurate information about nearby hospitals, including contact details, available departments, and emergency services.

    We also offer symptom-based search to help you find the right medical department for your specific needs, along with basic first aid information while you're on your way to get professional help.
